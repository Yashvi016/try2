Arunachal Pradesh Development Report

2000–2024 Comprehensive Analysis

This research project provides an in-depth examination of Arunachal Pradesh's development trajectory over two decades, analyzing key indicators across multiple sectors.

Key Components:

  • Comparative Development Analysis: Benchmarking against national averages and neighboring states
  • Sector Growth Evaluation: Assessment of agriculture, infrastructure, education, and healthcare
  • Policy Recommendations: Evidence-based suggestions for sustainable development

Methodology:

Utilized secondary data from government sources, applied time-series analysis, and employed comparative economic frameworks to derive meaningful insights.

Impact of Short-Form Digital Content on Attention

Behavioral Research Study

An exploratory study examining the relationship between short-form video consumption and attention span in young adults, with implications for educational engagement and cognitive development.

Research Highlights:

  • Survey-Based Research: Primary data collection from 200+ respondents across demographics
  • SPSS & Excel Analysis: Statistical analysis using correlation and regression models
  • Behavioral Insights: Patterns linking consumption habits to focus metrics

Key Findings:

The research revealed significant correlations between daily short-form content consumption and self-reported attention difficulties, particularly among 18-24 year olds.

India's Foreign Exchange Management Framework

Financial Policy Analysis

A comprehensive study of India's foreign exchange management policies, tracing the evolution from FERA to FEMA and analyzing the impact on economic stability and growth.

Study Components:

  • RBI Data Analysis: Examination of reserve movements and intervention patterns
  • Policy Phase Comparison: Pre and post-liberalization framework analysis
  • Financial Stability Evaluation: Assessment of crisis management mechanisms

Conclusions:

The transition to a more market-oriented forex regime has enhanced India's external sector resilience while maintaining policy flexibility for crisis management.
